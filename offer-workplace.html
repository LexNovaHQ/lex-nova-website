The Reality

Your team pastes a client brief into ChatGPT. Their confidential data is now in a training model you don't control. You deliver an AI-generated campaign. The client's legal team refuses to pay — because under Thaler v. Perlmutter, raw AI output falls into the public domain. You can't own it, and you can't sell it.

You cannot govern what you cannot see. "Shadow AI" is currently operating inside your company, creating massive undocumented liability. Without a formal policy, IP assignment chain, and human-in-the-loop protocol, every AI-assisted deliverable your team creates is a breach of contract waiting to happen.

The Solution

The Workplace Shield is a complete operational architecture built for teams using AI to deliver client work or build internal IP. We map the tools, assign the rights, and document the human contribution.

It establishes the "Traffic Light" tool policy, secures the copyright provenance, and implements the human-override protocols required by emerging state laws like the Colorado AI Act. Delivered as Review-Ready Drafts requiring only local counsel validation.
