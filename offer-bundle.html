The Reality

Most AI companies think about legal architecture in silos. The product team worries about ToS and agent liability. The ops team worries about data handling and employee AI use. Nobody is thinking about the moment those two exposure surfaces intersect.

That intersection is where the real liability lives. An enterprise client whose workflow broke because your agent failed will not stop at your product terms. Their lawyers will look at everything — how your team built the product, what data they used, whether your internal processes meet the standard of care you claimed in your commercial agreements.

Your diagnostic showed active gaps on both surfaces. A legal architecture that covers one and leaves the other open isn't architecture — it's a false sense of security.

The Solution

The Complete Stack combines The Agentic Shield and The Workplace Shield into a single integrated legal architecture — eleven documents covering every liability surface an AI company operating today actually faces.

Every document in the stack references the others. The DPA in your Agentic Shield informs the data handling protocols in your Workplace Shield. The definitions are consistent. The kill clauses are chained. This is not two separate products delivered together. It is one legal system built across two operational surfaces.
