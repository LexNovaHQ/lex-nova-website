Execution Protocol

How the Architecture Gets Built.

From intake to delivery, the Architect is in every step. Here is exactly what happens after you hit the Scanner.

Step 01

The Diagnostic

You complete the AI Risk Scanner—a 10-question diagnostic engine. It identifies your exact financial exposure based on active case law and prescribes the correct architectural kit before you pay a single dollar.

Step 02

Payment & Engagement

Once your exposure is mapped, you review the Engagement Terms and secure your build slot. Payment is collected via a secure gateway (PayPal/Stripe).

Step 03

The Vault & SLA Trigger

Payment confirmed. You enter your secure Client Portal. You submit your technical parameters into The Vault: LLM APIs in use, data flows, and jurisdiction. The 48-hour drafting SLA begins the moment The Vault is submitted.

Step 04

Architect Review & Logic Injection

The Architect reviews your Vault parameters personally and custom-injects the legal logic into your documents. This is bespoke architecture built on your exact operating reality, not find-and-replace templates.

Step 05

Draft Delivery

Your Review-Ready documents are unlocked in your Client Portal, accompanied by Architect Notes addressed directly to your local counsel. A personal video walkthrough explains the architecture in plain language.

Step 06

Revision Round

You review the architecture. If anything needs adjustment, you submit your notes via the Portal and the Architect revises within 24 hours.

Step 07

Local Counsel Stamp

You take the Review-Ready architecture to your local counsel. They review, validate jurisdictional compliance, and execute. The Architect Notes do the heavy lifting — your counsel stamps, not rebuilds. That is how we kill the billable hour.

Yes. Every Kit includes one round of async revision. Every Bundle includes two. Revisions are submitted via your Client Portal and completed within 24 hours. Additional revision rounds beyond what is included are available as a Change Order.
What payment methods do you accept? ↓

What happens after I pay? ↓
You log into your Client Portal and complete The Vault (the technical intake). The 48-hour drafting sprint begins the moment you submit The Vault. Documents, Architect Notes, and the video walkthrough are delivered directly to that portal.
What jurisdictions do you cover? ↓
We architect for global commercial markets — US, UK, EU, and cross-border operations. Documents are built to international commercial standards. Your local counsel handles the final jurisdictional validation in your specific operating territory.

Submit your intake and the Architect will review it personally. If your requirements exceed the Kit scope, you will receive a Scope Note explaining exactly what the Kit covers and what requires a Flagship upgrade. You will never receive a document that does not match your operating reality.

Yes. Laws change, enforcement phases roll out, and case law evolves. Our Liability Maintenance subscription ($297/month) exists specifically to keep your architecture current. Every client is offered this option upon delivery to avoid the need for manual Gap Reviews.
